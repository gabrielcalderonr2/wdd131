Interesting facts about Ecuadorian food

  • Ecuador has thousands of potato varieties — more than 4,000 recorded in the Andes.
  • Fine-flavored cacao native to Ecuador is used for high-quality chocolate worldwide.
  • Cuy (guinea pig) is a traditional celebration dish in the highlands.
  • Ingredients and techniques differ strongly between Coast, Sierra and Amazon regions.
