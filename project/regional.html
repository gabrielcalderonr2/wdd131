Coastal Region

The coast is famous for seafood dishes, bright citrus flavors, and tropical produce. Examples: ceviche, encebollado, bolón de verde.

Andean Highlands (Sierra)

The Sierra (highlands) is potato- and corn-rich: locro de papa, fritada, cuy on special occasions.

Amazon Region

The Amazon uses yucca, plantains, and local fish — often cooked in bijao leaves (maito) or grilled with local spices.
