Coastal Region

The coast is famous for seafood dishes, bright citrus flavors, and tropical produce.

Andean Highlands (Sierra)

The Sierra (highlands) is rich in potatoes and corn: locro de papa, fritada, and cuy on special occasions.

Amazon Region

The Amazon uses yucca, plantains, and local fish — often cooked in bijao leaves (maito) or grilled with local spices.
